Digital Twin for Project Construction Sites

vSite is a digital twin of your construction site. It combines all relevant project data and places it into its geospatial context to help all stakeholders have instant access to the information, plan better, collaborate more easily, and reduce project delays & rework.

Digital Twin in Augmented Reality

A high-level overview of the vGIS system. vGIS is designed for AEC companies, public utilities, municipalities, and service providers to aggregate and seamlessly convert 2D GIS, 3D BIM and Reality Capture data from Autodesk BIM360, Bentley iTwin, Esri ArcGIS, and other formats into powerful, accurate, and stable 3D visuals.
